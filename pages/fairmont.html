Enroll Now: Online Certificates & Courses for FRHI Employees

Click on a program area below to view and enroll in certificates & courses
How to Enroll
  • Please note that in order to be reimbursed for the cost of courses, you need to seek your manager’s approval prior to registering for courses. Please see your local Human Resources Department for further information about qualifying for or enrolling in eCornell programs.
  • New Students
    1. Select the course or certificate.
    2. Select the start date that best aligns with your schedule.
    3. Keep shopping if you want to add more courses.
    4. Checkout if you are ready to proceed.
      1. Create a new account.
      2. Follow the prompts to enter your email address and create a password.
    5. Complete your profile.
    6. Add your group code in the group code field about 3/4 of the way down the page. The FRHI group code is: G000445
    7. Complete your purchase.
    8. Check the screen and your email for your transaction receipt.
  • Returning Students
    1. Select the course or certificate.
    2. Select the start date that best aligns with your schedule.
    3. Keep shopping if you want to add more courses.
    4. Checkout if you are ready to proceed.
    5. Complete your profile.
    6. Note: You may need to add The FRHI group code for the credit card portal if it's not already listed in your profile. It is: G000445. You need this specific code listed in your profile to proceed and complete enrollment.
    7. Complete your purchase.
    8. Check the screen and your email for your transaction receipt.
